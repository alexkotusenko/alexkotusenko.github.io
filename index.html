Naloho - Cryptography Made Simple

What is Naloho?

Naloho is a website meant to educate people about basic cryptography and its uses in programming. This website touches upon different concepts, ciphers and cipher breaking methods within cryptography.

Naloho is a result of research done during the last year of the IB program (International Baccalaureate) at high school (also known as PÉI in French (Programme d’Éducation Internationale)), called The Personal Project.
